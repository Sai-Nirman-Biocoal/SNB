We Produce, Buy and Provide

Energy Solutions for steam purposes in Boilers, Furnaces, Heat Treatments, Casting, Ceramics and more.
01
Biomass Briquettes

We manufacture and provide Briquettes made out of corn, sawdust, soyabean, mustard, groundnut, baggase etc. with Diameter of 90 mm, Ash Content <10%, Moisture 8%, GCV 3600 ± 200 at an average.

02
Coal

Both Indian and Imported Coal available in various categories: Low GCV (3800 Gar), Medium GCV(4200 Gar), High GCV(5000 Gar), all of which are available in three size categories: 0-10 mm, 10-25 mm, 25-50 mm.

03
Bed Material (Refractory Sand)

Bed Material for Boilers of size 0.5 - 2.5 mm as per the customer requirement. Temperature: 1400° C.

04
Alum

We provide Phosphoric/Non-Phosphoric, Ferric/Non-Ferric Alum in both Powder and Lump form with both hydrate/non-hydrate base as per the customer requirement.

05
Charcoal

Wood Charcoal available in all sizes and in both powder and stem form as per the requirement.

06
Biomass Pellets

Our Biomass Pellets are made out of Sawdust(70%) and Baggase(30%) having Diameter 6-14 mm, Moisture 8% and GCV 3800 ± 200.
